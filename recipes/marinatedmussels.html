Marinated Mussels

Description

Mussels marinated in white wine - French style

Ingredients

  • 30g butter
  • 15cl dry white wine
  • 1 teaspoon flour
  • parsley
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 4 liters mussels
  • 2 shallots

Steps

  1. Chop the shallots.
  2. Scrape well and wash the mussels. Put them in a casserole dish with 1 knob of butter, the chopped shallots and the white wine.
  3. Open them in the covered casserole dish over high heat for a few minutes. Stir 2 or 3 times during cooking.
  4. As soon as they are open, remove the mussels from the casserole, keeping the cooking juices. Place them in a deep dish and keep them warm.
  5. Return the juice to the heat. Mix with a fork 1 teaspoonful of flour with the same volume of butter or margarine. Add everything to the juice from cooking the mussels on the fire. Let boil for a moment. Salt and pepper.
  6. Pour over the mussels. Sprinkle with chopped parsley and serve.
